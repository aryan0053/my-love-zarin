Forever in My Heart

A farewell to the most beautiful soul

You are the best girl anyone could ever ask for. The way you care, the way you love, the way you make everything feel so warm and real… it’s something I’ll always be grateful for. You came into my life and made it brighter in ways I never thought possible.

I’m really sorry… but I have to leave. Not because I want to, but because sometimes life doesn’t go the way we wish it would.

Just know this — no matter where I go or what happens, I will always love you. You’ll always have a special place in my heart that no one else can ever take.

I love you, Zarin.

Take care of yourself… you truly deserve all the happiness in the world.
